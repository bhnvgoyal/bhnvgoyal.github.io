Languages

We offer language services in the following Asian languages:

Chinese (中文)

Chinese is the language having the most number of native speakers, with more than one-fifth of the world’s population speaking it. Although there are numerous dialects of Chinese, the most important amongst them is Putonghua or Mandarin, which is based on the Beijing dialect. more…

Japanese (日本語)

The Japanese language is spoken by the approximately 130 million inhabitants of Japan and Japanese emigrants living abroad(especially USA and Brazil). Japanese is considered to be one of the toughest languages, as it is written using three systems of orthography or scripts: Hiragana, Katakana and Kanji or Chinese characters. more…

Arabic (العربية)

Arabic is one of the widely spoken languages in the world with over 300 million speakers. It is spoken in 24 countries and is the official language of all the countries of northern Africa, the Arabian peninsula, and in much of the Middle East, including Iraq, Syria, Lebanon, Kuwait, Bahrain and Saudi Arabia. As the religious language of Islam, it is also the second language of millions of Muslims around the world. more…

Indian Languages

India with a population of 1.21 billion has 22 official  languages. Half of these languages are spoken by more than 25 million people each. The most widely spoken language in north India is Hindi, which is based on the Delhi dialect. It belongs to the Indo-Aryan language family and is closely related to Urdu. The other important languages of the Indo-Aryan family are Punjabi, Gujarati, Marathi, Oriya, Assamese and Bengali more…
